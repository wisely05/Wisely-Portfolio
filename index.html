About me

Balancing film, live events & media studies

I'm Wisely T Daniel Roy, currently studying BSc Computer Science and Media Production at Karunya University. I love building visuals from scratch – planning shots, operating camera, and pushing footage in colour grading until it actually feels like the song or the moment.

I also lead live productions, especially youth and worship events – handling camera placement, directing a small team, and making sure the livestream looks intentional, not random.
